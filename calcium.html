Machine learning for network inference from neuronal recordings

This research is still in the works, so stay tuned for more updates or get in touch if you are curious about this one. Briefly speaking, as the schematics shows, we developed a method that can infer neuronal connections in live organisms from the time-series of the calcium flurescence recordings of their brains. While we write the paper, you can learn more on this work from this video recordings of a talk recently given by one of our collaborators in this project.
